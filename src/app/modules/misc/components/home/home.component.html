Tired of lining-up or waiting at the service area?

Create your own ticket in just a few seconds. We will take care of letting you know when your turn is close. We do care about your precious time!

Save Time

You are the owner of your time with this system. Feel free to leave the waiting area after you create your ticket . You will receive a notification when your ticket is close to be called.

Real Time Ticket Status

Check the status of your ticket anytime. You will see how many tickets are in front of you, and what is the average waiting time.

Prioritization

If your ticket falls under the priorities of the day, it will be moved to a closer spot in the queue.
